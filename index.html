About Me

Hello all! My name is Bobby Frazette Jr. I am a current student in the Rutgers University Coding BootCamp program. I am making a career switch into coding from my current background in operations because I have a passion for technology and I really enjoy the coding culture. I plan to learn all I need to succeed in the world of web development and beyond in this course and start a new career that I will enjoy and grow with for decades to come.


Prior to my career in operations, I attended Marist College in Poughkeepsie New York where I received my Bachelor's in Business Administration, Finance, and Psychology. I plan to use the skills I have learned in my prior education and work history to help me excel in my new career.
